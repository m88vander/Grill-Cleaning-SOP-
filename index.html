PURPOSE OF THIS SOP

To establish a safe, consistent, and thorough process for cleaning, inspecting, and preparing the outdoor grill for the next guest or resident use. Every step must be completed in order, and no grill is returned to service until it passes both the final inspection and the operational check.

📝 Notes
IF ANY COMPONENT FAILS THE OPERATIONAL CHECK, REMOVE THE GRILL FROM SERVICE IMMEDIATELY  •  Report the issue to your direct supervisor for repair or further inspection.
